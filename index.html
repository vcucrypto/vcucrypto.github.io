I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Virginia Commonwealth University.
From 2010-2013, I was a postdoc at the University of Maryland, under the direction of Jonathan Katz.  In 2010, I received my PhD at the University of Connecticut with Aggelos Kiayias as advisor.

My research interests include: Cryptography; Cryptocurrency/Blockchain Technologies; Information Security; Distributed Computing.
A project ongoing.
